Examples of Valid
Variable Names

Variables are a central component of programming and they need to be taken seriously. By coming up with clear, concise, and consistent variable names, you can make communication between yourself and developers more efficient, and your code labels easier to interpret and understand.

Choosing sensible names will save you time and energy later. You'll beable to figure out, from the name, what a certain variable, function, orclass represents. You'll also avoid using inappropriate names that mightresult in errors that are difficult to debug.
